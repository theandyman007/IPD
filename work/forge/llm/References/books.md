These are some accessible books about Moral Foundation Theury (MFT) and game theory including the iterated prisoner's dilemma (IPD) game.

Axelrod, R. (1984). The Evolution of Cooperation. Basic Books.
Haidt, J. (2012). The Righteous Mind: Why Good People Are Divided by Politics and Religion (1st edition). Vintage.
Johnson, S. (2004). Emergence: The Connected Lives of Ants, Brains, Cities, and Software. Scribner.
Ostrom, E. (2015). Governing the Commons. Cambridge University Press.
Ridley, M. (1998). The Origins of Virtue: Human Instincts and the Evolution of Cooperation. Penguin Publishing Group.
